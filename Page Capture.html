How It Works

Paste the URL

Enter any webpage URL. Click "Open" to launch it in a new tab so you can navigate to the exact view you want.

Share Your Tab

The browser will ask you to pick a tab or window to share. Select the tab with your target page and click Share.

Interact and Record

Scroll, click, hover, play — everything on the shared tab is captured in real time as a video.

Download Instantly

Hit stop, preview your recording, and download the video file. Everything happens locally — no server ever sees your data.

PageCapture runs entirely in your browser. No data leaves your device. Built with the Screen Capture API and MediaRecorder.

This page uses Google Analytics for anonymous usage statistics only. No personal data, URLs, or recordings are sent to any server.
